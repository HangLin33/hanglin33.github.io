THE WHISPER OF TIME

2026

《蝶恋花·茉莉渡江》

金风秋雪戏杨柳，万缕千丝，守乌纱发白。 共韵以观茶道砚，墨语沉香许流芳。 烛灯摇曳暖寒楼，日影微旧，长似水无穷。 愿往皓月同西东，曲书不赋天下人。

Jasmine Crossing the River

Autumn winds and falling snow tease the willows, Threads upon threads, guarding hair turned white. In shared rhythm, we watch tea and ink together, Words of fragrance sink deep, promising lasting echoes. Candlelight sways, warming the cold chambers, Sun shadows age softly, flowing without end. May we face the bright moon, east or west alike, Writing no songs for the world but only for ourselves.

2025

《日暮行·白梅冠雪》

夜阑流萤，昙花端安侍雨 枫秋晚亭，门阙怜忘清曲 归潮邃凌，岸舟侧问浪语 溯墨古今，朝颜古墙题序

White Plum Crowned with Snow

Fireflies drift through the late night, Ephemeral blossoms rest quietly beneath the rain. Autumn maples surround the evening pavilion, Palace gates listen to forgotten melodies. Tides return, deep and distant, Boats at the shore ask the waves their language. Ink traces past and present, Morning blooms write their names upon ancient walls.

2024

《何以歌·海棠葬雨》

褴褛着，韵观砚行孤墨 磷灯祝，往皓月随阡陌 傲雪醉，映万家如星落 鸿雁至，扬海浪化烟波

Begonia Buried in Rain

Ragged verses linger, Ink travels alone across the inkstone. Phosphor lamps offer silent prayers, The bright moon follows winding paths. Proud snow falls, A thousand households scatter like stars. Wild geese arrive, Lifting ocean waves into mist and smoke.
